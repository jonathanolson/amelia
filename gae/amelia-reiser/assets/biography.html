Amelia Reiser has a BFA in Dance from the University of Georgia, and is a native of Norman, OK, where she began her dance training under the guidance of Julie Blacksten in 1990.

While at the University of Georgia, Amelia has been involved in the CORE Concert Dance Company under the direction of Bala Sarasvati, the UGA Ballet Ensemble, and performed and choreographed for the Young Choreographerâ€™s Series: Emerging Choreographers and Senior Exit Concerts. Amelia attended the University of Oklahoma in the spring of 2009 where she studied Horton technique under Derrick Minter.

While in Oklahoma, she also trained and performed in trapeze dance with Perpetual Motion Modern Dance out of Oklahoma City. In 2010, Amelia worked with Diavolo Dance Theater and performed AWOL as a part of Company B in Athens, GA. Amelia has expanded upon her dance training at various festivals including Bates Dance Festival and The American Dance Festival.

Amelia is currently working with UGA faculty Rebecca Enghauser, Janet Robertson, and Darly Foster on the Spring Dance Concert 2011.
